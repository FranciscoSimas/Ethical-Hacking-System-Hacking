Gameplay

Elden Ring combines the intricate mechanics and deep difficulty levels that FromSoftware is known for. The gameplay focuses on open-world exploration, strategic combat, and character progression.

Combat

The combat system in Elden Ring is highly tactical, requiring players to carefully manage their stamina, use a variety of weapons and spells, and time their attacks and dodges perfectly.

Exploration

The game features a vast open world with dynamic weather and day-night cycles. Players can traverse the landscape on foot or horseback, discovering hidden dungeons, challenging enemies, and valuable treasures.
