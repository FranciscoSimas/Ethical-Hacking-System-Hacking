Lore

The lore of Elden Ring is deeply rooted in the mythos created by George R. R. Martin and Hidetaka Miyazaki. The world is filled with ancient deities, mystical creatures, and a rich history that players can uncover through exploration and interaction with NPCs.

The Elden Ring

The Elden Ring is a mysterious and powerful artifact that plays a central role in the game's story. Its shattering has caused chaos and strife throughout the land, and players must seek to restore it to bring order back to the world.

Characters

The game features a wide array of characters, each with their own backstories, motivations, and quests. From ancient warriors to enigmatic sorcerers, the cast of Elden Ring adds depth and complexity to the game's narrative.
