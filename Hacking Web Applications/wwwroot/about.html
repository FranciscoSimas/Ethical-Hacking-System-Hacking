About Elden Ring

Elden Ring is a highly anticipated action RPG from the renowned developers at FromSoftware. Known for their critically acclaimed titles such as Dark Souls and Bloodborne, FromSoftware continues to push the boundaries of the genre with Elden Ring.

The game is set in a fantastical world created through the collaboration of Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R. R. Martin, bringing together dark, intricate lore and immersive gameplay.
